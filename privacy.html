Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

ReddiSense collects minimal data necessary to operate:

Pro status — A local flag records whether Pro is unlocked. Pro is a one-time purchase; the license key is verified via Payhip. There is no account.

No browsing data — We do not collect, store, or transmit the URLs you visit, the stocks you look up, or your browsing history.

2. API Keys

If you use the AI summary feature, your API key is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device. It is never sent to our servers, never logged, and never shared with any third party.

3. Reddit Data

The Extension queries Reddit's public API on your behalf. Reddit's own Privacy Policy governs how Reddit handles those requests. We do not store Reddit data on our servers.

4. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or session recording tools.

5. Payments

Payment processing is handled by Payhip. We do not store your payment card details. Payhip's Privacy Policy governs payment data handling.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, email mumu@moonstack.org.
